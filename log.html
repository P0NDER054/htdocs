Even if I was offended by a father I didn't know well, or the emergence of a power of creation we don't always have control over, after careful study of spiritual teachings, I realize that we'll only be conscious of God if we clear away our anger and fear to experience God within. The Application of Mind Control

As early 1920: Swiss Scientist Rudolph Hess determined that he could induce various behavioral states by probing the brain with wires.

1953: Jose Delgado uses a radio controlled stimoceiver to stop a charging bull.

The Development of IMiDs

1953: The use of the IMID, Thalidomide is discovered in Switzerland.

1956-1960: Thalidimide is introduction and prescribed for widespread use in Germany.

1961: Birth defects are associated with it's use.

1964: A doctor in Jerusalem found a dramatic resolution of a lepra skin eruption upon administration of Thalidomide which was confirmed in 1965. The WHO published the results in 1971.

1998: The Steps Program is implemented to control and monitor access to Thalidomide.

2004-2010: Analogs of IMiDs are studied in clinical trials to treat resistant tumors.

About
