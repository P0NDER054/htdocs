Log and Report

We must regain the confidence of the public to ensure the safety of our managing venturers by bringing the truth to bear.

Archetype Development

When in doubt, prepare your media and materials more carefully. For example: when covering crime and complaints we need to use strenuous language more carefully and folders locked to preserve, protect and keep our work private.

Keep a Log! By logging entries on paper first and drafting public copy more carefully before going live, we're more likely to keep a reliable draft of the record as clear and present as necessary.

Focus Group

So far, we've gained competency at solving these primary problems:

  • Inflammation of the body: Inflammation from the stress of working in cramped spaces like a phone or tablet can be treated with: Bodywork
  • Inflammation of the mind: By dealing with anger, fear and even remorse, we literally burn up, but by using the tools we're continuing to develop, we can address the source of the aggravation: Options
  • Inflammation of each other: In order to resolve conflict and make amends we learn to Problem Solve.
